The sources identify several partners associated with the Association for Social Transformation and Human Advancement (ASTHA) and its projects. The primary partners include:
Strategic and Funding Partners
• Coforge Ltd: ASTHA’s main corporate partner for conducting Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) impact assessments and providing strategic inputs to increase project effectiveness.
• Chhaya Strategic Advisors (Chhaya): An associated consulting firm that works alongside ASTHA to provide strategic inputs, design monitoring systems, and carry out impact assessments.
• N G Paranjape Pratishthan (NGPP): A partner that supports specific initiatives, such as the E-Rickshaw service for school children in the Pasali Valley.
Implementing NGO Partners
The sources detail impact assessments for various NGOs that implement CSR projects supported by Coforge:
• BAIF Livelihoods-Karnataka: Focused on integrated livelihood approaches, including agriculture and livestock development.
• Jnan Prabodhini: Involved in rural development and joint projects with ASTHA, such as the Swadhar project in Velhe.
• Swayamsiddha (Swayamsiddha Institute): Specialized in women’s entrepreneurship and comprehensive village development.
• Udayan Care: Provides alternative care for orphaned and abandoned children (Udayan Ghars) and higher education fellowships for girls.
• CYDA (Centre for Youth Development and Activities): Focuses on youth empowerment and providing humanitarian aid to families affected by COVID-19.
• Vidya & Child: Works on the education of underprivileged children in slum communities.
• S.M. Sehgal Foundation: Partners on environmental and water management projects, such as Project Vardhan.
• Sparsha Trust: Dedicated to the holistic development and rehabilitation of vulnerable children and youth.
• Margadarshi (The Association for Physically Challenged): Supports the physical, social, and economic well-being of persons with disabilities.
• Ashagram (Param Prasad Charitable Society): Serves intellectually disabled and orphaned children and adults.
• SAFE (Social Action for Forest and Environment): Focuses on environmental conservation, such as restoring dumping sites into biodiversity parks.
• People for Animals (PFA): Dedicated to animal welfare, rescue, and rehabilitation.
• Voice of Stray Dogs (VOSD): Operates as a rescue and rehabilitation centre for stray dogs.
Other Notable Associates
• Xorient: A sponsor for providing solar-powered lighting in the Velhe Block.
• Maharashtra Bamboo Promotion Foundation: Collaborates on feasibility studies for bamboo plantation and processing.
